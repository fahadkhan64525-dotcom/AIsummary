# Demo Video Script

## Goal

Record a 2-4 minute walkthrough that shows the product as a polished AI startup app instead of a classroom prototype.

## Suggested flow

1. Open the landing dashboard in light mode.
2. Show the premium layout, metrics strip, summary workspace, history area, and document chat panel.
3. Toggle to dark mode.
4. Paste a short article into the text box.
5. Switch summary length from `Medium` to `Short`.
6. Change output language to another option such as `Hindi` or `Spanish`.
7. Click `Generate summary`.
8. Highlight:
   - title
   - overview
   - bullet list
   - section cards
   - extracted keywords
9. Use `Copy`, `Share`, and `Export to PDF`.
10. Ask a follow-up question in the document chat box.
11. Open the authentication modal.
12. Sign in or register.
13. Generate another summary and show it appearing in history.
14. Reopen a saved summary from the history sidebar.
15. End on the full dashboard view.

## Talking points

- “This platform accepts both pasted text and uploaded PDF files.”
- “The backend extracts and cleans document text before sending it into the summarization pipeline.”
- “Users can control length and language for different audiences.”
- “Every summary is rendered in a structured format designed for real stakeholder workflows.”
- “Saved history and document chat make it useful beyond a one-off summarizer.”
